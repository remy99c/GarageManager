## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 2, 2026**

**GarageMaster** is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our application handles your data.

### 1. Overview
GarageMaster is a local-only vehicle management application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

### 2. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect any personal data.** All information you enter into the application (vehicle details, maintenance logs, notes) is stored strictly locally on your device in an internal database.

### 3. Permissions
The application requires the following permissions to function:
*   **Camera:** Used solely for the purpose of taking photos of your vehicles or receipts to attach to your vehicle records locally. These images are not shared with us or any third parties.
*   **Storage:** Used to save vehicle images and the database file on your device.

### 4. Data Security
Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data relies on the security of your device. We recommend keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric lock.

### 5. Third-Party Services
GarageMaster does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

### 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
